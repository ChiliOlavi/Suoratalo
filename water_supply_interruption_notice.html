Water Supply Interruption Notice

Important Information for Our Guests

WATER SUPPLY TEMPORARILY INTERRUPTED

Dear guests,

Due to a sudden technical issue in the municipal water system, the water supply at Hotelli Suoratalo is currently momentarily off. We are truly sorry for the inconvenience this causes. Our team, including maintenance man Jari Salonen, is in close contact with the city and working hard to resolve the situation as soon as possible.

We understand that this affects your comfort, and we appreciate your patience. We will update you as soon as the water is running again. If you have urgent needs or questions, please contact the reception. Anna Korhonen is available at the desk 24 hours a day, even during this interruption.

During the water outage:
  • Toilets and showers are temporarily unavailable in rooms and the spa area.
  • Restaurant Imatra Bistro will serve a limited menu and offer bottled water.
  • Housekeeping and laundry services may be delayed.
  • For emergency assistance, please call +358 123 4567 or visit the reception.

We recommend checking our Current News and Announcements page for live updates.

Thank you for your understanding and Finnish patience. We know this is not ideal, but we’ll do our best to keep your stay as comfortable as possible. If you would like to discuss your booking or need help finding alternative solutions, please don’t hesitate to ask.
