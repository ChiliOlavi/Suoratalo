Updates from the Hotel

  • TVs and Technology:
    Our younger guests have discovered the TVs and media devices in the rooms (sometimes even before the adults). If you need help, see Television and Media Device Instructions.
  • Financial News:
    We believe in being open: early-year income has been lower, about 30% down since 14.3.2021. We’ve already reduced staff costs as much as possible, but it’s tricky – you can’t force people to buy things, and cold summer days don’t help. If you have suggestions, let us know!
  • Opening Hours:
    Changing opening hours too much confuses everyone (including us). We do our best to keep things stable, but please check Poikkeukselliset Aukioloajat for any changes.
  • Cleaning and Maintenance:
    More events mean more cleaning – Pekka is working hard, but please help us keep the WC areas tidy. Extra paper and cleaning costs do add up. For more info, see Cleaning and Housekeeping.
