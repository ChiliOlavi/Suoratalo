Event Booking and Ticket Sales

Are you planning to attend an event at Hotelli Suoratalo or looking for a cosy venue for your own happening? Whether you’re gathering with friends, hosting a business group, or just want to experience something special in Imatra, we make booking and ticket sales as easy as possible—though, as always, with our own Suoratalo style.

How to Book and Buy Tickets

All event bookings and ticket purchases go through our reception. Anna Korhonen is happy to help you at the desk, by phone (+358 123 4567), or by email (suoratalo@suoratalo.fi). We keep things simple—just let us know the event and the number of people, and we’ll write it down in our trusty booking table. For every Vellamo show or other happening, there’s a dedicated spot in our Kanlio folder (it’s a bit old-fashioned, but it works for us).

Maximum group size: 72 persons (according to our Xardo license)

Typical event time:
Mostly Saturday evenings in Hentalia, starting at 20:00. Duration about 1.5 hours.

Ticket Sales and Revenue

We sell all tickets at the reception. Ticket revenue is handled by Aino Himanen. If you’re an organiser, our team will make sure your sales are recorded clearly—no confusion. For all food and drink sales during events, revenue goes to Hotelli Suoratalo.

Special competitions, like KirskaTic, may offer a few tickets with a room included—ask for details!

Booking for Companies

Want to book an event for your company? Our meeting rooms and Imatra Bistro are available for private use. We also offer Arctinus-marketing packages if you wish to add a professional touch to your event.

How to Reserve

  • All reservations through the reception (Contact Us)
  • We note bookings in our event table and Kanlio folder—each Vellamo show gets its own entry
  • For larger events or special requests, contact hotel manager Matti Virtanen

More Information

For details on event times, prices, and special offers (like KirskaTic competition tickets), just ask at the reception. We promise to answer as soon as possible—even if it takes a moment to find the right folder.
