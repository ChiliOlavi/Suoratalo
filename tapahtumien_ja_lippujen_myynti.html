Upcoming Events at Hotelli Suoratalo

We host a variety of events, especially on Saturday evenings, mainly in our cosy Hentalia event room. Whether it’s a springtime performance, a local band, or a quirky competition like KirskaTic, you’ll find something to enjoy. Our events usually last about 1.5 hours and start at 20:00—perfect timing for a relaxed evening after a day in Imatra.

Ticket Sales & Reservations

We like to keep things simple. All reservations and ticket sales go through our friendly front desk—just talk to Anna Korhonen or call us at +358 123 4567. You can also email your request to suoratalo@suoratalo.fi.

  • Maximum capacity: 72 people (Xardo permit approved)
  • Ticket sales: We handle the ticket sales. For most events, ticket revenue goes to the event organiser, Aino Himanen. All food and drink sales during the event support our hotel.
  • Special packages: For our KirskaTic competition, there are a few tickets available with a room included!
  • Bookings: All event bookings are recorded by our reception team and carefully listed for each Vellamo show.

How to Book

  • In person: Visit our 24h reception (Anna is usually there—sometimes with coffee).
  • By phone: +358 123 4567
  • By email: suoratalo@suoratalo.fi

If you’re booking for a group or a company, let us know in advance so we can arrange everything smoothly. We recommend booking early, as our events are often popular among locals and guests alike.
