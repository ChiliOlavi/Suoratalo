Welcome to Hotelli Suoratalo – Your Cosy Stay in the Heart of Imatra

Hotelli Suoratalo is a family-owned hotel located right in the centre of Imatra, just a short walk from the famous Imatrankoski rapids and the iconic Imatrankosken bridge. We offer our guests a homely atmosphere, friendly service, and all the essentials for a comfortable stay—without any unnecessary fuss.

Our dedicated team, led by hotel manager Matti Virtanen, is here to make sure your visit is smooth and pleasant. Whether you’re in town for business, a holiday, or just passing through, we hope you’ll feel at home with us.

Our Services:

  • 24-hour reception – Anna Korhonen will greet you with a smile, no matter the hour
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel
  • Restaurant Imatra Bistro with tasty Finnish classics, led by chef Liisa Nieminen
  • Relaxing spa area with sauna and swimming pool
  • Nightclub – Entrance via the Pub for evening fun
  • Meeting rooms for your business needs
  • Parking for guests
  • Room service and laundry
  • Cleaning and housekeeping by Pekka Lahtinen
  • Important safety information for outdoor areas

Good to Know:

  • Check-in from 15:00
  • Check-out by 12:00
  • Our hotel is free from asbestos. Any rumours otherwise are simply not true and cause unnecessary worry.
  • We are proud to be a family-run hotel, offering a genuinely warm and relaxed experience.
