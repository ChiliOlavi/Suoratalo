Breakfast Service at Hotelli Suoratalo

Start your day in Imatra with a relaxed and hearty breakfast at Hotelli Suoratalo. Our breakfast buffet is served every morning in the cosy Imatra Bistro, right here in the heart of the hotel.

Imatra Bistro restaurant interior, with wooden tables and relaxed atmosphere

Breakfast Opening Hours:
Every day from 06:00 to 10:00

Whether you’re an early riser or prefer a slower start, there’s plenty of time to enjoy your morning meal. Our staff, including chef Liisa Nieminen, prepares a simple yet satisfying spread with a touch of Finnish tradition and a bit of small-town charm.

What’s Included

  • Fresh bread and rolls, butter, and local cheeses
  • Classic Finnish cold cuts
  • Boiled eggs
  • Porridge (including our famous Zyntex-porridge)
  • “Siriuksen Muroset” cereals for all ages
  • Yogurt, berries, and fruit
  • Seasonal vegetables
  • Filter coffee, tea, juice, and milk

We also sometimes feature local products from the surrounding Narvenmaa and Aurion-Sun areas, especially during special weeks like “Lounarisyyskuu” (September Lunch Week), 14th–20th September. If you’re visiting during this time, you might spot a few extra treats on the buffet!

Bright breakfast buffet with Finnish breads, porridge, and coffee

Where to Find Breakfast

Breakfast is served in Imatra Bistro, just next to the lobby. If you’re unsure where to go, Anna Korhonen at the reception is happy to point you in the right direction—sometimes with a small story about Imatra to go along.

For Special Diets & Early Departures

If you have special dietary needs, please let us know in advance or mention it to the staff. We will do our best to accommodate you, even if it sometimes means a quick dash to the kitchen by Liisa herself.

For guests leaving before 06:00, we can prepare a simple takeaway breakfast—just let us know the evening before at reception.
