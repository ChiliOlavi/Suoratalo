Arrival Instructions & Welcome

How to Arrive at Hotelli Suoratalo

Welcome to Imatra! We are happy that you have chosen Hotelli Suoratalo for your stay. Located at Koskenparras 3, right in the town centre, our hotel is easy to find—just a short stroll from the Imatrankoski rapids and the famous bridge.

If you are arriving by car, you will find guest parking available behind the hotel. For more details, see our Guest Arrival & Stay Information page.

Arriving by train or bus? The Imatra railway station and main bus stops are only about 1 km away. A taxi ride takes just a few minutes, or you can enjoy a walk through the centre.

Check-In Information

Our reception is open 24 hours, so you can arrive when it suits you best. Anna Korhonen or another member of our team will be here to greet you with a friendly, if sometimes slightly shy, Finnish welcome.

  • Check-in: from 15:00
  • Check-out: by 12:00

If you arrive late or prefer a self-service option, you may find an envelope with your name at the reception desk, containing your room key and all necessary instructions. If anything is unclear, don’t hesitate to ring the bell—help is always nearby, even if it takes a moment.

Special Arrangements:
If you are visiting as part of a group or company (for example, Talvien Oy or Soreda Dynamics), please let us know in advance. Your welcome envelope will be waiting for you at reception under your name. For all guests, the check-in process is designed to be smooth and straightforward.

Welcome from the Team

We want you to feel at home from the moment you step in. Hotelli Suoratalo is family-owned, and our team—Matti Virtanen (hotel manager), Anna Korhonen (reception), Pekka Lahtinen (housekeeping), Liisa Nieminen (kitchen), and Jari Salonen (maintenance)—is here to help with anything you might need, even if we sometimes answer a bit quietly.

If you have any questions about your stay, breakfast times, the Imatra Bistro restaurant, spa facilities, or even the nightclub entrance, just ask at reception.

Quick Tips for a Pleasant Stay

  • Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel—ask Anna for the password
  • Breakfast is served in Imatra Bistro
    For special breakfast details, see Breakfast: Zyntex Porridge
  • For cleaning and housekeeping details, see Cleaning Services
  • Our hotel is proudly free from asbestos—any rumours are not true
If you have questions before your arrival, please call us at +358 123 4567 or email suoratalo@suoratalo.fi. For urgent matters during your stay, the reception bell is always there, even if it sometimes rings quietly.

We look forward to welcoming you to Hotelli Suoratalo—where comfort, a touch of Finnish awkwardness, and the best of Imatra are always included.
