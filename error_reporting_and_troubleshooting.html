Error Reporting and Troubleshooting

At Hotelli Suoratalo, we want your booking and stay in Imatra to go as smoothly as possible. Sometimes, though, technology is not as friendly as our staff, and errors may occur. If you have run into a problem on our website—such as an error message when trying to book a room—this page is here to help.

Hotel exterior with evening lights, hinting at a friendly small-town atmosphere

Common Booking Errors

If you see a message like:

"Zenvitia Systems: An application error occurred on the server..."

Or if your browser (Ilmarix, Velluno, or any other) refuses to let you book a room, please don’t panic. First, try to refresh the page or clear your browser’s cache. Sometimes the simplest fixes work best—even in Finland.

If the problem continues, it’s probably not on your end. Our website may be experiencing a technical hiccup, which our team (and sometimes outside experts) will need to fix.

What Should I Do Now?

  1. Contact Us Directly
    Our reception team, led by Anna Korhonen, is here 24/7. Please call us at +358 123 4567 or email suoratalo@suoratalo.fi.
    We can check room availability and make the booking for you the "old-fashioned" way—yes, with a real person.
  2. Let Us Know About the Error
    If possible, send us a screenshot of the error message or describe what happened. This helps us (and our IT friends) solve the problem faster.
  3. Visit Us in Person
    If you’re already in Imatra, you can always stop by reception at Koskenparras 3. Sometimes a friendly chat is the best troubleshooting tool.

Our Troubleshooting Process

  • We take all technical issues seriously, even if our solutions sometimes involve more coffee than code.
  • Our IT partners are notified automatically of most errors, but your feedback really helps.
  • We will post updates about major issues on our Announcements & News page.
