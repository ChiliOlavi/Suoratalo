Guest Arrival & Stay Information

Welcome to Hotelli Suoratalo! We hope your journey to Imatra has gone smoothly and that you settle in comfortably. On this page, you’ll find all the practical information you need for your arrival and stay with us.

Arrival & Check-In

Our friendly receptionist, Anna Korhonen, is usually available at our 24-hour reception to greet you, but should you arrive during a rare moment when the desk is unattended, please don’t worry! There is a self-check-in option available—just follow the instructions at the entrance, or call us at +358 123 4567 if you need assistance.

Your room is located on the floor noted in your booking confirmation, and you’ll find your room number there as well. If you are unsure, Anna or another staff member will be happy to help.

Upon arrival, please fill out the passenger identification card placed in your room folder. Return it to the reception at your convenience. If you need to check out before reception opens or before you see Anna, just drop your room key through the slot at the reception desk.

Parking

Guest parking is available just behind the hotel. Access the lot from Koskenparras 3. If you arrive late and the main doors are locked, you can enter using your room key card—simply hold it against the reader and the door will click open.

Breakfast

Start your day with a taste of Finland! Our breakfast is served in the Imatra Bistro as a relaxed, buffet-style offering. We recommend trying our special Zyntex-porridge. Breakfast is available from 7:00 to 10:00.

If you prefer to have breakfast in your room or on the go, just ask at the reception for a takeaway option.

Spa & Relaxation

All guests have access to our spa area with sauna and swimming pool. Nothing beats relaxing in a warm sauna after a day exploring Imatra or attending meetings.

Check-Out

Check-out time is 12:00 noon. If you need a late check-out, just ask Anna at reception—we’ll do our best to accommodate you. For information on cleaning and housekeeping during your stay, see Cleaning and Housekeeping.

Emergencies & Help

In case of emergency or if you get locked out, please call our main number: +358 123 4567. Our hotel manager, Matti Virtanen, or maintenance man Jari Salonen, will come to help—even if it takes a few moments. The emergency contacts are also listed in your room folder and at the front desk.

More Information

  • For house rules, please see the information folder in your room.
  • For current news and announcements, check our notice board or visit our news page.
  • For important safety instructions outdoors, please read our guidelines.

If you have any concerns, suggestions, or encounter any issues, you can let us know directly or use our Error Reporting and Troubleshooting page.

We wish you a restful and pleasant stay at Hotelli Suoratalo! If you need anything at all, don’t hesitate to ask—sometimes we might look a bit serious, but we are always happy to help.

Kind regards,
Anna Korhonen and the whole Suoratalo team
