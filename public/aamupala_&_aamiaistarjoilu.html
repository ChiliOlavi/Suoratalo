Breakfast & Morning Offerings

Aamupala & Aamiaistarjoilu

Start your day in Imatra with a proper Finnish breakfast at Hotelli Suoratalo. Our breakfast is served in the relaxed Imatra Bistro, where you can enjoy both local flavours and familiar classics in a homely setting. Whether you’re heading out for a day of adventure or just want a slow morning, we have something for everyone.

Breakfast buffet with Finnish delicacies.

Breakfast Times

Tuesday–Friday:
Breakfast is served from 7:00 to 10:00.
We call this time “Kellan aamu” – the golden morning. It is a good moment for coffee, porridge, and planning your day in Imatra.
Weekend and Monday breakfast times may differ—please check Exceptional Opening Hours for up-to-date info.

What’s Included?

Our breakfast includes:

  • Freshly baked bread and rolls, local rye bread
  • Cheese, ham, and seasonal vegetables
  • House-made jams and spreads
  • Boiled eggs
  • Fresh fruit and yoghurt
  • Coffee, tea, and fruit juices
  • And, of course, our famous Zyntex porridge, made by our kitchen team led by Liisa Nieminen

We strive to use local ingredients whenever possible. If you have allergies or special dietary needs, just let us know—our staff is happy to help, even if we sometimes need to check twice.

Enjoy Breakfast Your Way

  • Early riser or late sleeper? If you need to have breakfast earlier or later, please ask at reception (Guest Arrival & Stay Information) and we’ll do our best to accommodate.
  • Room service breakfast is available—please order the night before.
  • For groups and business travellers, ask about our special breakfast packages (Meeting Room Bookings).
