Guest Satisfaction Survey – Hotelli Suoratalo

We at Hotelli Suoratalo want to make sure your stay in Imatra feels as easy and comfortable as possible. Your feedback helps us improve and keep things cosy and friendly for everyone. This survey will only take a few minutes, and as a small thank you, every guest who returns a completed survey can enjoy a complimentary cup of coffee or tea at Imatra Bistro. Just mention your feedback at the restaurant!

If you have any questions about the survey, just ask our hotel manager Matti Virtanen or reception’s Anna Korhonen. We are always happy to listen, even if your feedback is a bit awkward (we are Finnish, after all).
