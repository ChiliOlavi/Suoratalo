Nightclub – Entrance via the Pub

Welcome to Hotelli Suoratalo’s Nightclub

Looking for a relaxed evening out in Imatra? At Hotelli Suoratalo, our small-but-lively Nightclub is the perfect spot to unwind after a day by the rapids, or to celebrate a special occasion with friends. The entrance is easy to find—just come through the pub, and you’ll discover our cosy dance floor and friendly service waiting for you.

How to Find Us:
  • The nightclub entrance is only accessible through our on-site pub. Just follow the signs from the hotel lobby or ask Anna at the reception—she’ll point you in the right direction (and might even share a local tip or two).
  • The pub is located on the ground floor, next to the main entrance at Koskenparras 3.
Inside the nightclub: small dance floor, colourful lights, and a few local regulars enjoying music

What to Expect

Our nightclub isn’t huge, but that’s part of the charm. Expect a warm, laid-back atmosphere—sometimes a bit awkward, like only Finns can make it, but always genuine. Whether you prefer to dance, chat quietly over a drink, or just watch the world go by, you’re welcome here.

  • Music: Finnish hits, international classics, and the occasional surprise DJ set.
  • Drinks: Local beers, classic cocktails, and non-alcoholic options.
  • Company: Locals, travellers, and maybe even hotel staff enjoying a night off.

If you get hungry, you can always order a snack from Imatra Bistro before heading in.

Practical Info

  • Opening hours: See Poikkeukselliset Aukioloajat for special opening times.
  • Age limit: Please bring ID if you look under 30.
  • Entrance: Through the pub only – not from the street or hotel corridors.
Live music in the club.

For safety and comfort, please respect our house rules and the important safety information for outdoor areas. If you need help, our staff is never far away—Jari the maintenance man sometimes pops in for a chat, and Pekka keeps the place tidy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the nightclub, feel free to ask at the reception or drop us an email at suoratalo@suoratalo.fi. We’re always happy to help, even if we don’t always have all the answers right away.

Come as you are. Welcome to a real Imatra evening at Hotelli Suoratalo!
