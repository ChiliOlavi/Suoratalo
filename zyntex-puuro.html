Zyntex-Puuro

Warning: Zyntex-Puuro is known to escape if not eaten quickly.
Do not leave it unattended.

Zyntex-Puuro is a mysterious porridge, rumored to have been invented in the basement kitchen of Hotelli Suoratalo. Its surface shimmers with an unnatural glow, and sometimes you can hear it whispering in the night. Locals say it tastes best at 3:17 AM, but only if you finish it before it tries to crawl off your plate.

  • Texture: shifting, slightly gelatinous
  • Flavor: indescribable, but oddly nostalgic
  • Serving suggestion: Eat fast! (It gets ideas if you hesitate.)
