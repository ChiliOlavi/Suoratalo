Cleaning Services & Housekeeping

At Hotelli Suoratalo, we take pride in keeping our hotel clean and comfortable for every guest. Our housekeeping is handled by Pekka Lahtinen, who does his work with a careful Finnish touch—never too flashy, but always reliable. We keep things simple and honest, just like at home.

Daily Cleaning

Your room is cleaned every day to make sure you have a fresh and pleasant stay.

  • Floor mopping: All guest room floors are mopped daily with a damp cloth. We use trusted products like Sinitrio and Keltavio.
  • Bathroom cleaning: Toilets are scrubbed, floor drains are brushed, showers are rinsed and cleaned, and all surfaces are wiped down. For bathroom cleaning we use ORG SiroClean Oy’s ValoSuper, and for surfaces and the remote control, we use EtäOhjas.
  • Remote control wiped: Yes, even the remote control gets a careful cleaning.
  • Wiping surfaces: Table tops, shelves, walls, skirting boards, window frames, and radiators are all wiped.
  • Lampshades: Dust is removed from lampshades (but never with a wet cloth—just gentle dry wiping).
  • Lobby toilets: Women’s and men’s lobby toilets are cleaned daily using Tasalahti Clean and Cif Power and Shine.
  • Linen change: Fresh linen is provided, and used linen is rolled away for washing.
  • Sauna area: When the sauna is in use, it is cleaned thoroughly with Tervahuukun Pesugeeli for sanitary spaces and Löylykivi Pro for the benches.

Weekly Cleaning

Once a week, we pay extra attention to the shared spaces:

  • Vacuuming hallways
  • Dusting baseboards in corridors
  • Mopping stairs and wiping stair railings

Monthly & Occasional Cleaning

Some jobs don’t need to be done every day, but we still keep them on our list:

  • Washing room windows as needed, depending on the season and weather, using Kideklin Lasispray.
  • Extra bedding cleaning: Duvets and pillows are sent to the laundry for a deeper clean when needed.

A Few Notes

  • If you have any special cleaning requests or notice something that needs attention, just tell Pekka or let the reception know. We want you to feel comfortable.
  • Sometimes, depending on the situation, even the bedding (duvets and pillows) is sent for professional cleaning.
  • Our cleaning products are chosen for both efficiency and safety.

For questions about cleaning, lost items, or extra towels, please contact the 24-hour reception or ask Pekka directly if you see him in the hallway—he’s sometimes a bit shy, but always helpful.
