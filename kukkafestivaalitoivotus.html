Joyful Flower Festival Greetings from Hotelli Suoratalo!

Hotel entrance decorated with colourful summer flowers

Imatra blooms in summer, and so does Hotelli Suoratalo! We warmly welcome all guests and locals to enjoy the cheerful atmosphere of the annual Flower Festival. Whether you’re here to admire the blossoms around the city or just passing by on your way to the Imatrankoski rapids, we hope you’ll take a moment to stop, breathe in the fresh scent of summer, and feel at home with us.

Our family-run hotel loves to celebrate the small joys of the season. During the festival, you’ll find fresh flowers brightening up our lobby and Imatra Bistro restaurant. Chef Liisa Nieminen has even added a few floral touches to the menu—ask her for today’s special if you’re feeling adventurous!

Imatra Bistro table set with wildflowers and local treats

If you’re in need of a quiet spot to relax after the festival, our spa area with sauna and pool is the perfect place to unwind. And don’t forget, Anna Korhonen at reception is always happy to point you toward the best flower displays in town (or just chat about the weather, if you like).

We wish all our guests and neighbours a joyful Flower Festival! May it be full of colour, laughter, and gentle Imatra sunshine.

If you want to know more about what’s happening during the festival, check our Current News and Announcements page. For any questions about your stay, see Guest Arrival & Stay Information.

With best wishes from all of us at Hotelli Suoratalo,
Matti Virtanen, Anna Korhonen, Liisa Nieminen, Pekka Lahtinen & Jari Salonen
