Ready-Made Package Sales Report

Overview of Package Sales – 14 May 2022

At Hotelli Suoratalo, we occasionally offer ready-made packages for guests looking for a little extra during their stay in Imatra. Below is a summary of package sales and our experience so far.

Packages Offered

  • Sun Spa Package
  • Lakefront Golf Package
  • Summer Theatre Package
  • Lumijärvi Fishing Package

All packages can be booked directly through our reception—usually with Anna Korhonen, our ever-helpful receptionist (Guest Arrival & Stay Information). Full payment is required at check-in.

Package Booking and Payment

Guests have been able to reserve packages directly with Hotelli Suoratalo. Payment is straightforward: you pay the full amount at check-in. For some special packages (like the Lumijärvi option), guests can book in advance or upon arrival, with the ticket for external services provided at reception.

Sales Summary

To be honest, package sales have been quite modest. For example, during the sunny month of June 2023, not a single fishing package was sold. The most popular option has been the summer golf package, but even that has only sold fewer than 30 times per summer.

If you are interested in any of our packages, please ask at the reception or check the Ajankohtaista ja Tiedotteet page for any current offers.

Why So Few Sales?

We are a small, family-run hotel, and our guests often prefer to explore Imatra on their own. Still, we are happy to help organise activities if you are interested. Just let us know—sometimes Finnish modesty gets in the way of marketing, but we do our best!

Contact and More Information

If you have questions about packages, please contact hotel manager Matti Virtanen directly at suoratalo@suoratalo.fi or stop by the front desk.

For a full list of services, see our landing page or explore our spa area and restaurant.

Note:
There have been some confusing rumours about package sales and other hotels in the region. This report only concerns Hotelli Suoratalo, Koskenparras 3, Imatra. For official information, always refer to our website or contact us directly.

Thank you for your interest and understanding. We may not be the most aggressive salespeople, but we are always happy to welcome you with a smile!
